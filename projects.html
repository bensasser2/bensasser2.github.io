Internship Projects

As part of my internship, I have been part of three group projects with other students in the internship. The various goals and ideas of the projects are all unique in that they each pertain to a different field of computer science work.

Development

The first project in which I participated was a development project. The goal of the project was to redesign a website for the fictional company "MAXX Power" to have a higher visual appeal and smoother functionality. My assigned role was that of Quality Assurance Specialist, meaning that my job was to test the website's features and see what needed to be fixed. I was also tasked with writing policies for future Quality Assurance Specialists.

Security

My second project related to security. The goal of this project was to decide on a password management system for the "MAXX Power" company and implement improved password policies. My role was that of System Administrator, which typically would mean making sure the company's servers are functional and assisting with IT work. Due to the scale of this project, my role did not come into practice like in the development project. Rather, I served as the team lead for this project, coordinating our efforts and making sure all our work was done on time to be presented.

Data

My third and final project involved working with data. This project's goals were to find a way to gather data on the MAXX Power website and collect basic information, and to choose a way of organizing this data in reports. Half my group worked on the first portion, while I and another group member worked on the second portion. My role for this project was that of Policy Administrator, which, in practice, would create and enforce policies for effective data management. However, as with Security, because of the scale of the project, my project work did not involve these tasks. My jobs were more generalized, with my main contribution being the writing of an implementation plan document for the chosen data deporting tool.
